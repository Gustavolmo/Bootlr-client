Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Bootlr's Privacy Policy. At Bootlr, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users. This Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our AI-powered shopping search and assistant.

Information We Collect

As you interact with our service, we collect information about your usage patterns and preferences to improve our algorithm and provide a personalized experience. However, we do not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or payment details since our website does not include a sign-in or login functionality.

Use of Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience by remembering your preferences and collecting analytics to help us improve. Cookies are small data files stored on your device. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Data Security

We implement various security measures to ensure the safety of the information collected. However, please note that no method of transmission over the Internet is completely secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or relevant regulations. We will notify you of any significant changes and indicate at the top of the policy the date it was last updated.

Google AdSense

We utilize Google AdSense to display ads on our website. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your previous visits to our site and other websites on the internet. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our sites and/or other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Ads Settings. Additionally, you can opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalized advertising

Contact Us
